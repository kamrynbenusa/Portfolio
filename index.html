GIS Projects Here are a few examples of work I have completed using ArcGIS Pro.

Predicting Urban Development in Dakota and Scott Counties

In this project, I was asked to develop my own GIS problem using skills learned in class. I created a suitability model to determine areas within Dakota and Scott Counties that are likely to be developed within the next 20 years. This model considered multiple variables and the final suitable regions were decided based on analysis of urban development over the previous 20-year period (2000-2020).

Locating an Optimal Location for a Fire Station in St. Cloud

This project involved performing a series of network analyses that a GIS analyst for a city might be expected to perofrm. In this analysis, I quantified current fire department travel time performance, identified an optimal location for a new fire station, quantified future travel time performance should the new fire station be built, and current and future impacts on fire department travel time to the new Tech High School.

Details

Interpolating a Mass Balance Surface at Storglaciären

In this project, I employed spatial interpolation to estimate the total net mass balance of the Storglaciären glacier using data gathered from ablation stakes. This required an understanding of geostatistical techniques and concepts (Bayesian kriging, spatial autocorrelation, variograms, normality, et cetera). The analysis of glacial change demonstrates the possibility of using spatial data analysis to understand the impacts of climate change.
